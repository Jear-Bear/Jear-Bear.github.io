about me

Hi, I'm Jared.

A Japanese learner, YouTuber, and student-maker. I build things late at night that nobody asked for (yet).

I run @jareddesu on YouTube, where I document my journey learning Japanese through AJATT-style immersion. About 12K of you have decided to come along for the ride. I'm grateful you're here.

When I'm not making videos, I'm usually in front of Anki, working on hako — a small wooden box that shows your study progress on an LED matrix — or breaking some piece of software in a way that sends me down a rabbit hole.

Outside of that, I spend most of my hours on language learning, content creation, and whatever side project has captured my attention this month.
